Wuwana

A modern directory to help aspiring entrepreneurs to find suppliers.

View Wuwana Github Repo

My interest in tech started with the Wuwana project. The objective is to help individuals find local suppliers.

The problem lies in the fact that many suppliers do not have strong search engine optimization (SEO) strategies, making it difficult for them to rank high on search engines and attract potential customers. As a result, it can be difficult to find suppliers that offer the right products and services, at the right prices and with the right quality standards.
